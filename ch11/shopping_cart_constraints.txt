# Shopping Cart Checkout Test Model (with Constraints)

Member: Normal, VIP, Company
Payment: Card, ATM, StorePay, COD
Shipping: Delivery, StorePickup, SelfPickup
Coupon: None, Discount, FreeShip, FullDiscount
Invoice: Two, Three, EInvoice


# Constraints
IF [Shipping] = "SelfPickup" THEN [Payment] <> "COD";
IF [Member] = "Company" THEN [Invoice] = "Three";
